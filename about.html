about me

""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

Hello there, welcome to the personal website of Tom Hackshaw.

I've had a fascination with the Web and technology from a young age, and got to know all kinds of different people through IRC channels. At any given moment, I could be chatting to someone three times my age, on the other side of the world.

I am of the belief that the World Wide Web is a genuine social accomplishment and that we, as custodians of the Internet, should take care of it. I am reluctant to use monolithic platforms, particular social media, as I feel these are walled gardens or closed silos.

This site is a labour of love. It isn't the most flash or professional looking, but something that I tend to often. I can continually add to it, change things, and watch it grow. I consider it to be akin to a house, one that I maintain and welcome guests into.

If you like anything on/in this site, this house, feel free to take and use it for yourself. All of my text and images are freely licensed cc by-sa, and any code you are welcome to use for yourself too. In the case where some code (e.g. jQuery) is not mine, then remember to check their respective licenses.

Noho ora mai,

Tom
