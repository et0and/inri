Aotearoa Artist DB2019

An ongoing open source list of past and present artists from Aotearoa, New Zealand. I felt that the current list of artists available online on diÉerent sources were inadequate for NCEA and art school students, so built a simple static site resource from the ground up to help students get an idea of what kind of artist models they wanted to use.

The Language of Art 2019

An open source online resource for Art History / Fine Arts students with detailed examples and explanations of art terminology and conventions. Built using simple HTML, CSS and Javascript, the site acts as a comprehensive "one-stop-shop" for understanding key terms associated with art, and how to put this into practice when writing an essay.

HTML5 + CSS Javascript/ES7 language.art
